Henry Birge-Lee

Research Software Engineer

Henry Birge-Lee is a research software engineer focusing on network security and networking. He is lead author of the paper that invented MPIC and he is a founder/lead developer of the Open MPIC project. He also co-authored the MPIC requirements adopted by the CA/Browser Forum and is a member of the forum as an Interested Party.
